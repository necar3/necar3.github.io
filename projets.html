Oasis
The aim of this project was a complete rebranding of the company Oasis. I gave it a new clean and minimalistic look. An eco-friendly package using stand up pouches was chosen because it costs less to produce, and it's lighter and easier to recycle.
Arm & Hammer
I rebranded the logo and the packaging of the Arm & Hammer product. During the rebranding process, I modernized the logo while maintaining the familiar colour and hue of the product. In keeping true to its roots, I have maintained its so called “Cow Brand” through integrating the cow’s pattern into my design. Vignoble du Virelangue
This concept of wine bottle labels involves the idea of buyers being challenged with tongue twisters. The label provides an enjoyable experience throughout the consumption of the product.
Pomme Qualité Québec
This project won first place in the Pommes Qualité Québec contest. I reinvented the plastic bag by making it stretchable to reduce the plastic used for production. To improve user experience I added a handle which lacks in the current packaging.

Éclaté
This project is a flavoured popcorn packaging project. The packaging doubles as a bowl for a better user experience. The flat bottom of the expanded package enables it to stand on its own and the tabs on the sides are used as handles. Each flavour is representative of a movie genre.
Anticosti
Due to humidity, sea salt tends to clump together and they harden. The solution I came up with is a squeezable plastic packaging that retains its shape. The clear plastic shows the product inside.
